Welcome to The GoldenEye Depot

This site is an effort to collect and curate information about GoldenEye 007 for the Nintendo 64. There's a lot of good information about GoldenEye on the internet already, but there's also some bad information and speculation presented as fact. In addition many fan sites have gone offline over the years. This site's goal is to be an accurate and reliable source for anyone who wants to learn about GoldenEye.

Most screenshots on this site are made with the 1964 GEPD Edition emulator. It's pretty accurate and makes high resolution screenshots possible. There are a few niche situations 1964 doesn't handle well such as Line Mode. In those cases I use the ares emulator.

GoldenEye Quick Facts

  • Released in 1997 for the Nintendo 64.
  • Sold over 8 million copies.
  • Third-best-selling N64 game.
  • Received 1998 BAFTA Interactive Entertainment Award.
  • Developed by Rare and a core team of about 9 people in rural England.
  • 20 single player levels.
  • 2 to 4 players multiplayer mode.
  • Hugely influential on first person shooters that came after it.
