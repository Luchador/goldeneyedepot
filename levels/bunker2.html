Bunker

Mission 5: Severnaya

Part ii: Bunker

Objectives

  1. Compare staff / casualty lists (SA / 00A)
  2. Recover CCTV tape (A / SA / 00A)
  3. Disable all security cameras (SA / 00A)
  4. Recover Goldeneye operations manual (00A)
  5. Escape with Natalya (A / SA / 00A)

Background

Escape is the first priority. Find the staff and casualty lists, and the CCTV tape of your capture. If there are any witnesses, try and bring them out as well.

M
