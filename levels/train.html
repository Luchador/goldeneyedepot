Train

Mission 6: St. Petersburg

Part v: Train

Objectives

  1. Destroy brake units (A / SA/ 00A)
  2. Rescue Natalya (A / SA / 00A)
  3. Locate Janus secret base (SA / 00A)
  4. Crack Boris' password (00A)
  5. Escape to safety (A / SA / 00A)

Background

The leader of Janus, Alec Trevelyan, is holding Natalya hostage on his armored train.

Q

A train, 007, pretty simple. Destroy the braking units in each carriage and the emergency systems should bring it to a halt.
