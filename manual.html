Game Boxes

NTSC (US) Box

Ah, how I miss physical media! This is a scan of my own GoldenEye 007 box. I know there's also a variant of this box that says "1997-1998 Game of the Year" on the front, but I haven't been able to find one in good condition yet.

You can tell the screenshots on the back are from a significantly earlier version of the game. The KF7 uses the old model, blood is more visible, and the Silo elevator is still there, among a number of other differences.

PAL (Denmark) Box

Many thanks to my Danish friend Haj who sent me these beautiful scans of his GoldenEye 007 box! This particular one has a sticker on the front with a phone number for the Danish Nintendo Club.

NTSC-J Box

This is a scan of my Japanese GoldenEye 007 box. The first thing I notice about this box is the front has a portrait orientation while the US and PAL versions have landscape oritentation. The screenshots on the back use the final KF7 model, meaning they must have been taken from a later version of the game than the shots used on the other boxes.

I do not speak any Japanese so I used Google Gemini to translate the blurbs on the box. If you do speak Japanese let me know how accurate this translation is.

Front of the box:

(Text on blue/green background) It'll make you shiver with the Rumble Pack!


(Main title) (Gooruden Ai) GoldenEye


(Small pink oval) 3D Gun Action


(Bottom center) Notice: This work is a work of fiction based on the movie of the same title and contains many shooting scenes.


(Bottom right) Rumble Pak Compatible with Backup Function (referring to the internal save battery)

Back of the box:

Tension is the ultimate entertainment. 3D Real Gun Action, arriving now.


007 = James Bond arrives on the NINTENDO 64, bringing with him the ultimate tension and intense impact. To succeed in the mission concerning the secret weapon "GoldenEye," you must explore 3D fields such as underground secret bases, the interior of military trains, snowy plains, and jungles. Missions filled with intense gunfights and the use of special items are always a brush with death. Your careful attention and accurate shooting skills will be what saves Bond himself. The life-and-death struggle in the 2–4 player battle mode will make you even hotter! Survive!


Left (Pink Image): An astonishing sense of realism; the exciting 3D field is the stage for your adventure!


Center (Teal Image): Utilize many types of guns to defeat powerful enemies equipped with high thinking skills!


Right (Hot Pink Image): The 2–4 player battle mode allows you to enjoy sweat-inducing tension right in the palm of your hand.
