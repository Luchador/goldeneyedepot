Pre-Release GoldenEye

This section is for the history of Goldeneye 007 prior to release. Back in the day people called this "beta GoldenEye." The game was in development for about two and a half years and it went through countless changes before release. A pre-release cartridge has never leaked so we dig into old videos, screenshots, and articles to find out what we can.

Documents
Early notes and design document.

Timeline

  • January 1995 — Programmer Mark Edmonds joins the team.
  • February 3, 1995 — First notes we have from GoldenEye's development.
  • April 1995 — The first article on a GoldenEye video game appears in Nintendo Power volume 71.
  • April 10, 1995 — Project Lead Martin Hollis prepares the game's Design Document.
  • First half of 1995 — Background artist Karl Hilton joins the team.
  • Spring of 1997 — Work begins on multiplayer. (Source)
  • August 23, 1997 — GoldenEye 007 released in Japan.
  • August 25, 1997 — GoldenEye 007 released in North America.
  • November 7, 1997 — GoldenEye 007 released in the UK.
